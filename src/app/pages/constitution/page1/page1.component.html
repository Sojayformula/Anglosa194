LA ANGLICAN 1 JSS 1994 OLD STUDENT

ASSOCIATION (ANGLOSA 194) CONSTITUTION

1.0

ARTICLE ONE - NAME

1.1

The association shall be known as La Anglican '1' 1994 Old Student Association hereinafter referred to as "ANGLOSA '194".

2.0

ARTICLE TWO - AIM

2.1

To support academicwork and other activities of our Alma-Mata, La Anglican 1 Junior High School, located at La in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana, and the livelihood of members of the year group.

3.0

ARTICLE THREE - OBJECTIVES

The group will fulfil the aim by:

3.1

promoting education, good health and wellbeing of the resident students in La Anglican 1 Junior High School, and working together as old student, irrespective of our ethnic origin, abilityt, sex, belief or political affiliation recognising the value of our Many differences

3.2

Involving students and necessary resources to improve students academic works

3.3

Carrying out and promoting practical conversation, to educate, encourage and support the resident as well as members of '194-year group'.

3.4

promotinggood-will of '194-year group'

3.5

Raising funds where appropriate to finance the work.

3.6

Opening a bank account for its operations.

3.7

Organising meetings, trainingcourses and events.

3.8

Working with similar groups and exchange information and advice with them.

3.9

Taking any action that is lawful, which would help the groupto fulfil its aims.

4.0

ARTICLE FOUR - MEMBERSHIP

4.1

Membership of ANGLOSA 194 shall consist of ordinary and Honorary Members.

4.2

Ordinary membership shall be open to all 1994 Old Student of La Anglican 1 JSS who completed the full academic year. A member shall be identified by all members and must have completed all the neccesary membership registration formalities and must have a membership card.

4.3

It is the responsibility of the secretary to register members with the association.
