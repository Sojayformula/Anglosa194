ASSOCIATION (ANGLOSA 194) CONSTITUTION

9.0

ARTICLE NINE - ELECTION OF OFFICERS

i. Election of executive members shall be held at an Annual General Meeting (AGM) and shall be by secret ballot.

ii. Executive members shall hold office for a term of two years and may be re-elected.

iii. Only members of ANGLOSA 194 in good standing shall be entitled to vote, propose, nominate or support the nomination of candidate.

iv. Outgoing executive shall handover to the incoming executive at the fourth quarter executive meeting.

v. The constitution shall mandate three (3) NON anglosa 194 members who shall constitute the electoral board to supervise the election.

9.1

QUALIFICATION OF CANDIDATE

A candidate for election to any position on ANGLOSA 194 shall:

i.

Be a member for more than one year.

ii.

Be a member in good standing.

9.2

NOMINATION OF CANDIDATE

i.

The nomination of a candidate shall shall be made by a member nominating him herself or another member.

ii.

Nominations of candidates shall open thirty days before election.

iii.

Where at the expirationof the time prescribed for nominations, only one candidate stands for an executive posituion, that candidate shall be declared duly elected by the electoralOfficer.

10.0

ARTICLE TEN - MEETINGS

10.1

The association shall meet once in nevery two months at a location to be determined by the members.

10.2

The association meetings must be chaired by either the President or the Vice-President.

10.3

If the President and the Vice-President are both absent, the presiding member for that meeting must be any member of the executive.

10.4

The Secretary shall give notice of meeting with an agenda at least 30 days before meeting convenes.

10.5

Quorum for meeting shall be eigth (8) members, of which three (3) shall be official representatives of executive, and no business except that of adjournment shall be conducted without without a quorum.
