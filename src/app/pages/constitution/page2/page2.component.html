ASSOCIATION (ANGLOSA 194) CONSTITUTION

5.0

ARTICLE FIVE - RIGHT OF MEMBERSHIP

5.1

A member may exercise the rights of membership when is or here name is entered in the register of memers.

5.2

A right of membership of the Association is not capable of being transferred or transmitted to another person and terminates on the cessation of membership whether by death, resignation or otherwise.

5.3

A member in good standing shall be entitled to one vote at any election of the Association. A member in good standing refers to one who is pontual and regular in activity and contributes to the growth of the group.

5.4

A member is not eligible to vote until three (3) General Meetings after he or she has been accepted.

6.0

ARTICLE SIX - ADMISTRATION

6.1

Members of ANGLOSA 194 Year Group shall be managed by OFFICERS not less than three (3) people and not more fifteen (15) members elected/selected and accepted by the group members at the group's meeting.
The OFFICERS shall be:

The PRESIDENT
The VICE PRESIDENT
The TREASURER
The SECRETARY

and such other officers, the group shall deeem necessary at the meeting.

6.2

The officers shall meet at least once a year, either through a face-to-face or by other electronics means.

6.3

The OFFICERS shall have power to set up sub-groups and working parties as deemed necessary who shall be accounted to the OFFICERS as well as the group.
