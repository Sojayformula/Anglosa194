La Anglican 1 JSS Year Group Association

The Anglican 1 JSS Year Group Association (Anglosa 194) is an association that brings together all “1994 student” of La St. Paul's Anglican 1 JSS and creates for them a platform that will promote their common interests and bring development and progress to La Paul's Anglican 1 JSS and quality education in Ghana, and at the same time provide for other related matters connected with the wellbeing of members, their associates.

La Anglican 1 JSS Year Group Website

We are delighted to launch the Anglosa 1 JSS official website as a safe space for our Old Scholars community to connect online with each other and the School. The site was initiated by the executive and the Members, and it is a pleasure to see the dream realised. I encourage Old Scholars far and wide to visit and enjoy making this site their own.
