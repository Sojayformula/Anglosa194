La Anglican 1JHS Year Group
Association

The Anglican 1 JSS Year Group Association (Anglosa 194) is an association
that brings together all “1994 student” of La Anglican 1 JSS and creates for
them a platform that will promote their common interests and
bring development and progress to La Anglican 1 JSS and quality
education in Ghana, and at the same time provide
for other related matters connected with the wellbeing of
members, their associates and La Anglican 1 JSS.

La Anglican 1JHS Year Group
Website

We are delighted to launch the MOSA portal as a safe space for our Old
Scholars community to connect online with each other and the School.
The portal was initiated by my predecessor and the Foundation trustees,
and it is a pleasure to see the dream realised. I encourage Old Scholars far
and wide to register and enjoy making this portal their own.
