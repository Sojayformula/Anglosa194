Our Mission, Vision and Aim

About Us

Our Mission

To promote the noble ideals of St. Paul's Anglican 1 JSS, in line with the aspiration of the founders which is to promote education and honour those who work for the promotion of same and to ensure the uplifting of 1994 old students.

Our Vision

"A highly respected year group, recognized both locally and internationally, dedicated to providing excellent support and promoting the wellbeing of its members. Our mission is to unite all 1994 alumni of La St. Paul’s Anglican 1 JSS."

Our Aim

As members of this year group, our aim is to support one another in learning, growth, and development. Together, we strive to bring out the best in each member’s potential. We encourage a positive attitude towards learning and teamwork, so that being part of this group is both enjoyable and rewarding, while also laying a strong foundation for lifelong success.
